About the author

Alan Wake is a New York native, spending most of his life within New York City with only the occasional summer trip outside to visit family. After graduation high school, Alan went on to attend college at NYU, receiving a bachelor's degree in creative writing. During his tenure at the prestigious university, Alan began submitting articles and short stories to several literary publications, many of which can be found online to read now.

After gradutation, Alan became a freelance writer, most notable for his work on some of the most memorable episodes of Night Springs. However, Alan would find steady literary fame in the release of his Alex Casey series, cementing his standing as one of suspense-thriller's most notable authors.

Alan is currently on vacation with his wife, but will resume the convention circuit when they return.
